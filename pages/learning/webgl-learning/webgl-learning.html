The Rasterization Rendering Pipeline

WebGL acts as an interface parsing client vectors into flat screen pixels via a sequence known as the rendering pipeline. Vertex transformations evaluate geometric constraints before fragment pipelines solve final per-pixel raster colors.

02

Shader Program Structures

WebGL programs require explicit compilation of two structural procedures written in GLSL (OpenGL Shading Language): The Vertex Shader controls coordinate positioning metrics, while the Fragment Shader sets pixel color codes.
